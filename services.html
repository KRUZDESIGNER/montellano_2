What is K-SPAN?:



• K-Span makes buildings with straight walls and vault type design.

• This new technology has revolutionized the industry of the construction.

• It´s amazing rapidity and very under cost is incomparable with any traditional or prefabricated system .
- Mobility: It is designed to be totally self-sufficient, relies on his own generator of energy which allows to take literally the factory to the site of construction.
- Rapidity: It is possible to make and construct a structure in one day.
- Simplicity: The structures of steel do not need clinches, spikes, screws or weld.

• The structure is placed simply in a base of concretly simple. The length of the structure is unlimited.
- Isolation: It relies on a cap that there provides the thermal necessary resistance, which gives it a major versatility for use in all the climates.
- Impermeability: This system of construction allows totally impermeable panels.
